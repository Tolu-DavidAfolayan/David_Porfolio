Telco Customer Retention

Key Business Takeaways
The Telco Customer Retention report delivers critical insights into why telecom customers churn and which user segments are most at risk. Built entirely in Power BI without any code, it empowers decision-makers with an intuitive dashboard to visualize churn patterns, identify high-risk profiles, and evaluate the revenue impact of customer loss. Segment-level analysis reveals that customers on monthly contracts and those using manual payment methods are more likely to leave. The report also highlights how tech support availability and customer tenure influence loyalty. These findings guided targeted retention strategies such as loyalty rewards and AutoPay enrollment, enabling marketing and customer success teams to reduce churn, boost engagement, and improve overall customer lifetime value...
